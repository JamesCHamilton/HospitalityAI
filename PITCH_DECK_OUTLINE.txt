HOSPITALITY AI - PITCH DECK OUTLINE
"Closing the 20-Day Referral Gap with Clinical Intelligence"

---

SLIDE 1: THE HOOK (Title Slide)
- Subject: HospitalityAI / EthicOS
- Subtitle: The End-to-End Orchestration Engine for Frictionless Referrals.
- Visual Idea: A clean, white medical interface showing a "Referral Authorized" checkmark.
- Content: Eliminating the administrative friction that delays patient care.

---

SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM (The Pain Point)
- Subject: The 20-Day Referral Black Hole.
- Content:
    * It takes an average of 20 days for a specialist referral to be authorized.
    * $1 Trillion in annual administrative waste in the US Healthcare system.
    * Process relies on manual faxes, outdated registries, and phone tag.
- Key Message: "Delayed care is denied care."

---

SLIDE 3: THE SOLUTION (The Product)
- Subject: HospitalityAI
- Content:
    * An automated pipeline that handles Intake, Matching, and Adjudication in seconds.
    * 1. AI Intake: Unsiloed AI parses messy PDFs into structured context.
    * 2. High-Fidelity Matching: Enriched provider data (Crustdata + NPI) for "Best Fit" results.
    * 3. Instant Authorization: AI Rules Engine auto-adjudicates claims based on clinical reality.

---

SLIDE 4: HOW IT WORKS (The Ingestion Layer)
- Subject: Beyond Simple OCR.
- Content:
    * We don't just read text; we extract clinical intent from clinical pdfs and client forms.
    * Using Unsiloed AI to transform unstructured fax documents into machine-readable JSON schemas.
    * Captures: Symptoms, Insurance, and Urgency.

---

SLIDE 5: OUR SECRET SAUCE (Data Enrichment)
- Subject: The Union of Two (The Source of Truth).
- Content:
    * Static registries (NPI) are often years out of date.
    * We join Federal Data with NYC Hospital Metadata + up to date NPI api-endpoints.
    * Result: We know not just where a doctor is, how they relate to what the patient needs.

---

SLIDE 6: THE REASONING ENGINE (AI Adjudicator)
- Subject: Clinical Logic at Scale.
- Content:
    * Built-in Rules Engine for:
        1. Network Adequacy (In-network verification).
        2. Step Therapy (Ensuring conservative treatments were tried first).
        3. Medical Necessity (AI triage based on Red Flag keywords).
    * Decision Logic: Confidence-based routing (Auto-Approve vs. Manual Review).

---

SLIDE 7: REAL-TIME OBSERVABILITY (The Dashboard)
- Subject: Millisecond Adjudication.
- Content:
    * Powered by Supabase Realtime.
    * Insurance providers see incoming claims appear and adjudicate live without page refreshes.
    * Dual-Output Strategy: FHIR JSON for the future, e-Fax Fallback for the legacy present.

---

SLIDE 8: THE IMPACT (Business Case)
- Subject: Efficiency for All Personas.
- Content:
    * Patients: Instant specialist booking and insurance certainty.
    * Providers: Clean, EHR-ready clinical briefs via FHIR.
    * Payers: Drastic reduction in manual review volume and administrative overhead.

---

SLIDE 9: FUTURE ROADMAP

- Subject: Make Healthcare Frictionless.
